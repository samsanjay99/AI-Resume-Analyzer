# User Guide - MY HISTORY Features

## 📚 MY HISTORY - Complete Guide

### Overview
The MY HISTORY page is your central hub for accessing all your past activities, analyses, and deployments. Everything you've done on the platform is saved and accessible here.

---

## 🎯 What You Can Do

### 1. 📊 View Analysis Reports
Access all your resume analyses (Standard, Smart, and Deep modes)

### 2. 📄 Download PDF Reports
Download professional PDF reports of any analysis

### 3. 🌐 Access Deployed Portfolios
View and visit all your deployed portfolio websites

### 4. 📁 Track Uploaded Files
See all files you've uploaded

### 5. ⏱️ View Activity Timeline
See your complete activity history

### 6. 📥 Export Data
Export your data as CSV or JSON

## 📖 Step-by-Step Guides

### How to Download Analysis Reports as PDF

#### Method 1: From MY HISTORY

1. **Login** to your account
2. **Click "📚 MY HISTORY"** in the main navigation
3. **Choose the analysis type:**
   - Click **"🔍 Analyses"** tab for Standard analyses
   - Click **"🤖 AI Analyses"** tab for Smart/Deep analyses
4. **Find your analysis** in the list
5. **Click to expand** the analysis details
6. **Scroll down** to the bottom of the expanded section
7. **Click "📄 Download Analysis Report (PDF)"**
8. **PDF will download** automatically to your Downloads folder

#### Method 2: From ANALYSIS HISTORY

1. **Login** to your account
2. **Click "📊 ANALYSIS HISTORY"** in the main navigation
3. **Click "📊 Detailed Reports"** tab
4. **Find your analysis** in the list
5. **Click to expand** the analysis details
6. **Click "📄 Download Full Analysis Report (PDF)"**
7. **PDF will download** automatically

### What's in the PDF Report?

**Standard Analysis PDF includes:**
- ✅ ATS Score
- ✅ Keyword Match Score
- ✅ Format Score
- ✅ Section Score
- ✅ Missing Skills
- ✅ Recommendations

**AI Analysis PDF (Smart/Deep) includes:**
- ✅ Resume Score
- ✅ Experience Years
- ✅ Detected Skills (up to 20)
- ✅ Education Information
- ✅ Projects (up to 5)
- ✅ Certifications
- ✅ Analysis Summary
- ✅ AI Feedback

### How to Access Your Deployed Portfolio URLs

1. **Login** to your account
2. **Click "📚 MY HISTORY"** in the main navigation
3. **Click "🌐 Deployments"** tab
4. **You'll see all your deployed portfolios** with:
   - Portfolio name
   - Status (Active/Inactive)
   - Deployment date
   - Live URL
   - Admin URL

5. **To visit your live site:**
   - Click the **"🌐 Visit Live Site"** button
   - Your portfolio opens in a new tab

6. **To manage your site:**
   - Click the **"⚙️ Manage Site"** button
   - Opens Netlify admin panel

7. **To copy the URL:**
   - Look for the code block with your URL
   - Click to select and copy
   - Share with anyone!

---

### How to View All Your Analyses

1. **Login** to your account
2. **Click "📚 MY HISTORY"** in the main navigation
3. **Choose a tab:**
   - **"🔍 Analyses"** - Standard resume analyses
   - **"🤖 AI Analyses"** - Smart and Deep AI analyses
4. **See summary table** with all analyses
5. **Click any analysis** to expand and see full details
6. **Download PDF** if needed

---

### How to Export Your Data

1. **Login** to your account
2. **Click "📚 MY HISTORY"** in the main navigation
3. **Scroll to bottom** of the page
4. **Choose export type:**
   - **"📊 Export Analyses (CSV)"** - All analysis data
   - **"🌐 Export Deployments (CSV)"** - All deployment URLs
   - **"⏱️ Export Timeline (CSV)"** - Complete activity log
5. **Click the button**
6. **File downloads** automatically

## 🔍 Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Where do downloaded PDFs go?
**A:** PDFs download to your browser's default Downloads folder.

### Q: Can I download old analyses?
**A:** Yes! All your analyses are saved permanently. You can download any analysis anytime.

### Q: What if my deployment URL doesn't work?
**A:** Check the status indicator. If it shows "INACTIVE", the deployment may have been removed. Contact support if needed.

### Q: Can I re-analyze an old resume?
**A:** Yes! Go to Resume Analyzer and upload the same resume again for a fresh analysis.

### Q: How long is my data stored?
**A:** Your data is stored permanently in our secure database. It will be available as long as your account is active.

### Q: Can I delete old analyses?
**A:** Currently, analyses are kept for your records. Contact support if you need specific data removed.

### Q: What's the difference between MY HISTORY and ANALYSIS HISTORY?
**A:** 
- **MY HISTORY** - Shows ALL your activities (resumes, analyses, deployments, files, timeline)
- **ANALYSIS HISTORY** - Focused specifically on resume analyses with more detailed views
